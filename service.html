Services Provided By Us

Following Types of services are provided

  • Inspection and verification of quality of goods /products , at all or any stage of the goods transaction to minimize risk due to wrong supply, delay damage ,loss etc .
  • Source verification – capability and capacity appraisal of the supplier’s vis-a vis buyer’s requirements.
  • Problems solving through tailor made solutions service , related to quality management , during procurement , production , processing, handling, Storage ,Transportation and shipping of bulk commodities and other products .
  • Lab testing and quality assessment of commodities and products.
  • Training and consultancy in as per total Quality Management( ISO/IEC 17025 : 2017) and ISO Quality system (ISO 9001 : 2015)

SECTOR SPECIFIC SERVICES

ENGINEERING PRODUCT AND PROJECTS:

  • Source verification , appraisal of Suppliers and Pre-qualification survey .
  • Quality Inspection , testing and checking of material / products before dispatch / shipping and supervision of loading.
  • Statutory and safety Certification related services . Competent person under petroleum act –Rule 126 & 130. Pressure vessel Inspection etc.
  • Inspection/ survey and Valuation of Vessels and Oil Rigs.
  • In-service and shut down inspection of plants .
  • Supervision and monitoring services during shut down maintenance of plants and Machinery .
  • Insurance Survey and Loss Assessment.
  • Inspection and testing of Explosives.
  • Non- destructive Examination (NDE) services.
  • Project Site supervision, Project Monitoring , Witnessing of commissioning Test etc.
  • Quality Assurance and Quality Control Services at shop Level.
  • Progress reporting and Expediting.
  • Engineering design review , review of drawings , specification and instructions to determine if they meet with the clients requirements as well as the applicable regulations.
