Know About Us

GEOCOAL ENGINEERING PVT LTD is a leading Indian Inspection, Testing and Certification Company in the Jharkhand Region in India. Established in the year 2012, our Company has already set up a comprehensive, meticulous and efficient services and systems network in various sectors such as Agriculture, Engineering, Minerals and Metals - both locally and on an international scale. More than 450 highly trained, dedicated and specialized workforces support us in all our endeavors across the country. Our Laboratory is certified by ISO 9001:2008 and also ANAS-BNR accredited certifier & NABL,NABCB certified. GECPL offer services that are Unique, Professional and Objective. We at GECPL do not manufacture, buy or sell (trade) or have any financial interest in any transaction thereby working extremely professionally and independently to ensure minimal risks to customers proactively.

GECPL professionalism is firmly anchored within our salient Core Values namely:

  • Impartiality
  • Independence
  • Integrity
  • Expertise
  • Quality of Service

GCEPL demonstrates commitment to impartiality by:

  1. Adopting policy statements that clearly express commitment.
  2. Providing a Confidential Declaration form for members of management and all other inspection personnel.
  3. Establishing procedures to ensure impartiality, evaluating risks and subsequent re-evaluation of risks (GCEPL/NABCB/MSP/01).
  4. Maintaining records of Risk Assessments and by implementing countermeasures to prevent or minimize the identified risk.
  5. Incorporating systemic steps, throughout the relevant policies and procedures, which minimize or prevent identified risks and expose situations when impartiality is compromised.
  6. Facilitating all personnel with access to top management to report incidents or behaviors supposed to compromise impartiality.
  7. Discussing impartiality in the Management reviews along with consideration of decision in the minutes of meeting followed by internal communication.

Our Mission
